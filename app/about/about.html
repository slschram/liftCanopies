ABOUT US

Welcome to Lift Canopies! We sell boat lift canopies at low prices. We're a father-son owned business based out of Minnesota, but we ship across the US. If you're in the market for a boat lift canopy or know of someone who is, take a look around our site.

All of our canopies have a 7-year limited warranty. They are made with 18oz PVC-coated high-tenacity polyester material that will not rot, shrink, or stretch and offers unparalleled adhesion to reduce wind whip. In addition, there are several colors to choose from to best fit your style.
